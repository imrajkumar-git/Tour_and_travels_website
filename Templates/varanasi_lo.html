3 Days - Varanasi

With the vibrant culture, lovely people, fascinating rites and rituals, the city of Varanasi is a spiritual bliss for every traveller. Trip Planner gives you a chance to be a part of the surreal beauty of this mystical city through our amazing Varanasi tour packages. This religious tour of Varanasi takes you to some of the top pilgrimage sites of the city that cannot be ignored while in the city. Bharat Mata Temple, Sankat Mochan, Annapurna Temple, Vishwanath Temple and Manas Mandir are some popular pilgrimage sites in Varanasi that you will be visiting on this expedition. Besides, the talk of this tour is the evening Ganga Aarti on the banks of River Ganges. Adding a different grandeur in this entire excursion is the early morning boating experience in the waters of Ganges. This Varanasi trip also covers the popular tourist places of Sarnath and Ramnagar Fort. Rest you can be assured by our remarkable services and great hospitality that includes accommodation, food and travel.


ITINERARY

    Day 1: ARRIVAL IN VARANASI

  • Your amazing Varanasi tour with Trip Planner will start with your arrival at Varanasi airport/railway station, whichever of your choice. At the station/airport, our representative will be waiting to escort you to a pre-booked hotel. Upon arrival, check-in to the hotel and get freshened up.Thereafter, you will be served with a delectable lunch. Followed by this, you will be taken out for a sightseeing tour of Saranath. It is a small village in the suburbs of Varanasi which is known as the place where Lord Buddha delivers his first sermon to his disciples. In Sarnath, you will visit some popular tourist sites of the region - Buddha temples, the Dhamekha stupa, Chaukhandi stupa and the archaeological museum. In the evening, you will be treated with the surreal sight of Ganga Aarti on the banks of River Ganges. For dinner and overnight stay, you will be transferred back to the same hotel.

    • Day 2: VARANASI TO RAMNAGAR

  • The day 2 of this wonderful Varanasi trip will start a bit early with an early morning boat ride on the waters of River Ganges. Visit all the popular Ghats of Varanasi through boat ride and also relish the spectacular views of people offering water to Sun God along with taking a holy dip in the river water.Thereafter, head back to the hotel. Upon arrival, you will be served with a delicious morning meal.Post breakfast, be ready for an another Varanasi sightseeing tour. On the tour, visit the 17th century Ramnagar Fort. Once the residence of Maharaja of Banaras, the fort has become an interesting sightseeing choice which also includes a historical museum. In the evening, you will be accommodated back to the same hotel.

    • Day 3: VARANASI SIGHTSEEING TOUR AND DEPARTURE

  • Enjoy a delectable breakfast in the morning.Followed by this, be ready for a final sightseeing tour of the city. On the tour, be ready to visit some tourism places of Varanasi - Bharat Mata Temple, Vishwanath Temple, Sankat Mochan (Hanuman Temple), Annapurna Temple, Banaras Hindu University (BHU) and Manas Mandir. After the sightseeing tour, you will be taken back to the hotel.Be ready with your backpack and check-out from the hotel to drive back to the airport/station. Upon arrival, board your flight/train back to either onward or home destinations.
