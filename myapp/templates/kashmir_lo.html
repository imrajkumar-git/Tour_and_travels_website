7 Days - Kashmir

The backdrop of snow-dressed mountains, land painted in green and stunning lake, perhaps, this is what strikes your mind on reading the term Splendor of Kashmir. Well, our Kashmir Holiday Package offers you exactly what you have been dreaming of. This 6 nights and 7 days tour package takes you to all the picturesque destinations of Kashmir like Srinagar, Sonmarg, Pahalgam and Gulmarg. On one hand in Srinagar the houseboat stay and a visit to the Mughal Gardens makes you feel content, and on the other hand the vistas of Sindh Valley and Sindh River in Sonmarg win your heart. Later, a tour of the calm and serene Lidder Valley at Pahalgam, and the high altitude excursion to Gulmarg make the tour more memorable. Thus, in a single tour package you get to experience a 360 degree view of Kashmir.
